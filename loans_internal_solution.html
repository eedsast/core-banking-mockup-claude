Loans - internal solution

Loan internal solution will be split on MVP part (happy path scenario) to support Equal instalment with 30/360 interst rate loan type and future development. Future development will cover all other Repayment schedule patterns, Interest rate methods and Fees.

To cover MVP Loan module we need:

1.     Loan Product definition - the database model should cover all future business requirements (not only OneFor). Full benefits of the model will not be visible in the MVP, but will be build in the future.

2.     Loan account application - the exisiting process will not be changed. In the future Loan application process will be developed as separate microservice. Additionally, Orchestration tool will be seleted to manage process.

3.     Repayment Plan calculation - supporting different Repayment schedule patterns (Equal instalments, Equal principal, Flat, Bullet payment) different interest rates (30/360, ACT/360, ACT/365, ACT/ACT), different irregular rates principles (interest onmly payable on the first instalment, adjustment on the last instalment, True equal instalment) and adjustment on the last instalment.

4.     Effective interest rate - Effective interest rate calculation.

5.     Decision engine and loan approval - the exisiting process will not be changed.

6.     Loan opening and Disbursement -

7.     Collections - Due amounts will be collected according to priorities defined on the Product level (Collection definition table) and transaction will update Loan_transaction, Loan_Collection and Loan_collection_details table.

8.     Daily accruals - Interest income a.k.a income amortization / rediscounting. Two types of Accounting methods will be supported Accruals (Daily accruals will be supported as MVP, montly accruals during in the future) and Cash based accounting (in the future).

9.     EoD Loan balance - EoD balance table will store all relevant loan balances for Reporting purposes.

10.  Penalty rate calculation and arrears

11.  Early repayment (only full Repayment/Pay-off will be possible in MVP).

12.  Reversal of transactions (with assumption that all transaction after reversed one should be deleted and executed again. This will be semy manual job).


Assumptions

Legend: processes with gray background wil not be changed during implementation

Model have to be built on the way that it is extendable without major change of logic.

MVP model limitations

There will not be possible to:

·        make refinance,

·        change of interest rate,

·        change of duration, i.e. anything what consider changing of Repayment Plan,

·        change instalment due date if it is due on weekend or holiday day.

MVP model will not support security deposits, variable interest rate, collaterals.

Only EMI - equal monthly installment loan types will be possible.

Only full repayment/payoff will be supported, no partial repayment.

Only daily accruals/rediscounting and penalty interest calculation ACT/ACT will be supported.

Only one model of collection priorities will be setup: 1. fee, 2. penalty interest, 3. interest, 4. principal (first closing older demands).

Migration approach

At the moment when new Loan microservice module will be fully functional we will already be in Production with current version of BNPL.

It means that we have two options for migration to new platform:

Option a) Without migration. At one point of time all new loans will be created in new microservice (not in Fimple). Old loans will leave in Fimple until end of lifecycle.

APIs for presentment on screens should be adapted to go and check in legacy system (Fimple) and new microservice.

Less risky.

Option b) With migration of data from Fimple.

Migration of loan data is risky job without exact knowledge of Fimple database.

If we engage Fimple for support it could be costly. This solution have to be tested during UAT period.
